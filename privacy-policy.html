Privacy Policy

Hayya - Your Islamic Companion

Effective Date: November 29, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Hayya ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience using our Islamic companion application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Location Data

Hayya collects location data to provide you with:

  • Accurate Prayer Times: We use your location to calculate precise prayer times based on your geographical position.
  • Qibla Direction: Your location helps us determine the accurate direction to the Kaaba in Mecca.
Important: Location data is processed locally on your device. We do not transmit, store, or share your location data with any external servers or third parties.

Data We Do NOT Collect

We want to be clear about what we do NOT collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Account or login credentials
  • Payment or financial information
  • Contacts or address book data
  • Photos, videos, or media files
  • Browsing history or app usage analytics
  • Device identifiers for advertising purposes

How We Use Your Information

The location data we access is used exclusively for:

  • Calculating prayer times specific to your location
  • Determining the Qibla direction from your current position
  • Providing prayer time notifications based on your local times

Data Storage and Security

All data, including your preferences and settings, is stored locally on your device. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • All calculations are performed on-device
  • No personal data is transmitted to external servers
  • Your preferences are stored securely in the app's local storage

Third-Party Services

Hayya may use the following third-party services:

  • Prayer Time APIs: We may fetch prayer time calculation data from Islamic prayer time services. Only your approximate location (city-level) may be sent to calculate times, without any personal identifiers.

Children's Privacy

Hayya is designed for general audiences and is not specifically directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age.

Your Rights and Choices

You have control over your data:

  • Location Access: You can enable or disable location permissions at any time through your device settings.
  • Notifications: You can manage prayer time notifications in the app settings or device settings.
  • Data Deletion: Uninstalling the app will remove all locally stored data.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top.
