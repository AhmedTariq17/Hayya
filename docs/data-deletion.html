How to Delete Your Data

Hayya respects your privacy and gives you full control over your data. Since Hayya stores all data locally on your device, you have complete control over your information.

Important: Hayya does NOT store any of your personal data on external servers. All your data (prayer statistics, tasbih counts, settings, and preferences) is stored locally on your device only.

Data Stored by Hayya

The following data is stored locally on your device:

  • Prayer time preferences and calculation method
  • Tasbih/Dhikr counter statistics
  • App settings (theme, time format, notifications)
  • Location data for prayer times (cached temporarily)

Delete All Data - Option 1: Uninstall the App

Steps to delete all your data:

  1. Go to your device's Settings
  2. Navigate to Apps or Applications
  3. Find and select Hayya
  4. Tap Uninstall or Delete App

This will permanently delete all data associated with the app from your device.

Delete All Data - Option 2: Clear App Data

Steps to clear app data without uninstalling:

  1. Go to your device's Settings
  2. Navigate to Apps or Applications
  3. Find and select Hayya
  4. Tap Storage
  5. Tap Clear Data or Clear Storage

Data Retention

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • We do not retain any copy of your data on our servers
  • Your data exists only on your device
  • Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all data
  • We cannot recover your data after deletion
