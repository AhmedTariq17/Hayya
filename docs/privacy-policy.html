Privacy Policy

Hayya - Your Islamic Companion

Last Updated: December 18, 2024

Introduction

Welcome to Hayya ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience using our Islamic companion application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Location Data

Hayya collects location data to provide you with:

  • Accurate Prayer Times: We use your location to calculate precise prayer times based on your geographical position.
  • Qibla Direction: Your location helps us determine the accurate direction to the Kaaba in Mecca.
Important: Location data is processed locally on your device. We do not transmit, store, or share your location data with any external servers or third parties.

Usage Data

We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve app performance and user experience. This includes:

  • App crash reports
  • Feature usage patterns
  • General engagement metrics

This data is anonymous and cannot be used to identify you personally.

Preferences

Your app preferences (notification settings, calculation method, display preferences) are stored locally on your device.

Data We Do NOT Collect

We want to be clear about what we do NOT collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Account or login credentials
  • Payment or financial information
  • Contacts or address book data
  • Photos, videos, or media files
  • Device identifiers for advertising purposes

How We Use Your Information

The information we access is used exclusively for:

  • Calculating prayer times specific to your location
  • Determining the Qibla direction from your current position
  • Providing prayer time notifications based on your local times
  • Improving app performance and user experience
  • Responding to customer support inquiries

Data Storage and Security

All data, including your preferences and settings, is stored locally on your device. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • All calculations are performed on-device
  • No personal data is transmitted to external servers
  • Your preferences are stored securely in the app's local storage
  • Data is encrypted during any transmission using SSL/TLS protocols

Third-Party Services

Hayya may use the following third-party services:

  • Prayer Time APIs: We may fetch prayer time calculation data from Islamic prayer time services. Only your approximate location (city-level) may be sent to calculate times, without any personal identifiers.
  • Crash Reporting: Anonymous crash reports may be collected to help us fix issues and improve the app.

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

Your Rights and Choices

You have control over your data:

  • Location Access: You can enable or disable location permissions at any time through your device settings.
  • Notifications: You can manage prayer time notifications in the app settings or device settings.
  • Analytics: You can opt out of analytics data collection in the app's Privacy Settings.
  • Data Deletion: You can clear all app data from the Privacy Settings screen, or uninstall the app to remove all locally stored data.
  • Data Request: You can contact us to request information about what data we have collected.

Children's Privacy

Hayya is designed for general audiences and is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our practices, please contact us at:

Email: hayyaprayers@gmail.com

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 48 hours.

Consent

By using Hayya, you consent to this Privacy Policy and agree to its terms. Thank you for trusting us with your Islamic practice journey.
